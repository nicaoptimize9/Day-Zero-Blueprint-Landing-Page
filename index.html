Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this program for?

Whether you have 10 physicians and only one location or 100 physicians and multiple locations, this program has been designed for healthcare leaders and physician practice owners who are passionate about creating success for their organization and their patients but want to do it while having time and energy to also focus on personal goals.

You've already tried multiple ways to create team engagement and improve processes but nothing seems to stick.

What's the time investment for this program?

You'll get about 1 to 1½ hours of short actionable videos each week which will guide you through how to create the team and organization you envision.

Plus, a 60-minute live strategy coaching session with Adrienne for each of the modules to help you refine and implement what you've learned from the modules.

Do I have to be a CEO or Physician-Practice Owner to join this program?

No! There are many different titles that we take on as leaders. I've worked with groups of all sizes and, as long as the leaders are engaged and aligned, we can create the transformation for your team.

My vision for my organization isn't clear. What do I need to work through before I join the program?

You're in the right place! This program has been designed to help you get clear on what you want to design for your practice, your team, and your role.

As long as you are open and prepared to do the work to unlock your team's potential, you are ready to succeed with this program.

When are the coaching sessions? I have a very busy schedule and I want to know if I'll be able to make it…

I understand! I'll be holding two calls every week to help with availability, one on Wednesdays and one on Thursdays. (Specific times will be set with participant input prior to kick-off of the program.)

All calls are also recorded so you can watch them later if you are unable to attend.

* There are also VIP options for separate calls for you and your team so please let me know if you are interested in this during our Day Zero Strategy Call.

Are there opportunities to ask questions or get feedback between coaching sessions?

Yes, you and your team members in the program will be part of a private, secure, online community where you can get feedback from Adrienne (and the other support coaches).

It's also a great place for you to learn from your peers in the program and share ideas and best practices so you can all grow together.

Do I get access to all the modules at once?

No, I've found that some team members will jump ahead while others will lag behind and I want us to stay in sync.

Each week a new module will be released, with short videos and action-focused tools and templates. The weekly strategy & coaching sessions will be focused primarily on the content for that week.

How many people on a team usually go through the program?

I've found that the best results occur when around 5 to 15 people from your organization's leadership team go through the program together.

This allows you to create a shared vision and begin to drive change quickly in an aligned way.

What are the steps to enroll in the program?

When you click 'Book a Strategy Call', you'll be able to schedule your Day Zero Strategy Call, where we will dive into your current state and begin crafting the vision for your organization's optimal future.

When you book the call, you'll be asked to complete a brief questionnaire that will help me audit your organization to help us maximize our call and determine if the program is the right fit for you and your team.
